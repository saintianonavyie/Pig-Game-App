READ AND CLICK GAME RULE TO HIDE ME:

  • - The game has 2 players, playing in rounds
  • - In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he whishes. Each result get added to his ROUND score
  • - BUT, if the player rolls a 1, all his ROUND score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn
  • - The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his ROUND score gets added to his GLOBAL score. After that, it's the next player's turn
  • - The first player to reach 100 points on GLOBAL score wins the game
  • - A player looses his ENTIRE score when he rolls 6 in a row. After that the next-player's turn.
  • - The input field is set with a number which a player must get as point inorder to win. if not setted, probably the default of 100 will auto-apply.
  • - The first to get to setted point is the WINNER if only he HOLD.
